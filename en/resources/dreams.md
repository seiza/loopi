**Dreams** is an incredible creative sandbox for PlayStation 4 and 5, where you can play, create, and share your own games, music, animations, and sculptures.

Developed by Media Molecule (the creators of LittleBigPlanet), it offers a visual logic system that allows you to "code" without typing a single line of text. You use "wires" to connect sensors, timers, and logic gates to characters and objects.

It is one of the most powerful creative tools available on a console, allowing anyone to bring their imagination to life, whether it's a simple platformer or a complex 3D simulation.
