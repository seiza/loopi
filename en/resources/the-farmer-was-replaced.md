**The Farmer Was Replaced** is a programming game where you automate a farm using a Python-like scripting language.

You start with a simple drone and must program it to plant, water, and harvest crops. As you progress, you'll unlock new tools and crops that require more complex logic and optimization. It's a charming and accessible introduction to coding and automation.
