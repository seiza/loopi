**Last Call BBS** is the final game from Zachtronics, presented as a collection of mini-games on a retro computer.

It includes several programming-focused puzzles like `20th Century Food Court`, where you design factory lines, and `X1000 Model 4`, which involves circuit board design. It's a nostalgic love letter to the early days of personal computing and logic puzzles.
