**Cubetto** is a wooden robot designed to teach children the basics of computer programming without screens. 

Inspired by the Montessori method, it allows the youngest children to learn logic through physical play. They control the robot by placing colorful wooden blocks on a special control board, representing simple commands like "forward", "left", or "right". 

It's a tactile and screen-free way to introduce the core concepts of algorithms and sequences.
