**Bits & Bytes** is a card game designed to teach children the basics of computer coding and algorithmic thinking.

The goal of the game is to guide your program through a series of obstacles to reach the "base." While playing, children become familiar with programming logic in a fun way.

### Concept:
The game helps children understand:
- **Instruction sequences**: planning moves in order.
- **Problem-solving logic**: finding the best path.
- **Core coding concepts** (bits, bytes).

**Ideal age:** 7+ years old
