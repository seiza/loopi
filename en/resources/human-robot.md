Coding is above all a way of thinking. These games help understand fundamental concepts without straining the eyes.

**The Human Robot:**

**The Principle:** One twin plays the "Robot" and the other the "Programmer". The programmer must give ultra-precise instructions to the robot to accomplish a task (e.g., brushing teeth, making a jam sandwich).

**The Lesson:** If the instruction is imprecise ("Put the jam"), the robot might put the entire jar on the bread. This teaches the surgical precision required for coding.
