CodeMonkey is an online platform that teaches computer programming to children. The game uses a fun and interactive story where a monkey has to retrieve its stolen bananas by solving puzzles with code.

As children progress, they move from visual blocks to real programming languages like CoffeeScript and Python. It's a popular choice for schools and home learning.
