**7 Billion Humans** is the sequel to Human Resource Machine, where you automate a massive group of office workers to solve puzzles inside a computer made of people.

Unlike its predecessor, you now control multiple workers simultaneously using a new language that introduces concepts like parallelism and synchronization. It's an excellent way to learn about multi-threading and distributed logic in a fun, visual way.
